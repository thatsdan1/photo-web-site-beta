Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is your late policy?

I allow a 15-minute grace period for all sessions. After that, a $15 late fee will be added to your total. Please plan accordingly to respect both our time.

2. What is your turnaround time for photos?

The standard turnaround time is 1 week. For special requests or expedited delivery, additional fees may apply depending on the request.

3. Do you require a deposit?

Yes. All bookings require a 25% non-refundable deposit to secure your spot.

  • For studio shoots, a $70 deposit is required.
  • The deposit goes toward your final total.

4. Can I cancel or reschedule my shoot?

Cancellations or reschedules must be made at least 24 hours in advance. Last-minute cancellations may result in the loss of your deposit.

5. Do you post unedited pictures?

No. Unedited pictures will not be posted or delivered. I take pride in delivering polished, high-quality edits only.
